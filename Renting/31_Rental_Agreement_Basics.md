# Renting: Rental Agreement Basics

## Overview
The Rental Agreement is the legal contract between you and the agency. In the UAE, this is a serious document linked to the RTA system. Signing it without reading can lead to unexpected liability. This guide highlights the clauses you must check before scribbling your signature.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Check-Out Report** | A diagram of the car where existing scratches are marked. **Crucial.** |
| **Salik Tag No.** | The unique ID of the toll sticker. Useful to verify if you are charged for the correct car's tolls. |

## Contract Checklist

### 1. Vehicle Condition Diagram
*   Look at the "Car Outline" drawing on the contract.
*   **Rule:** If a scratch is on the car but NOT on the drawing, ask them to mark it. **Right now.**
*   *Tip:* Take a photo of the signed diagram.

### 2. Time of Return
*   Agencies charge for a full extra day if you are 1-2 hours late.
*   Check the "Grace Period" (usually 1 hour).

### 3. Financials
*   Confirm the **Deposit Amount** matches what you paid.
*   Confirm the **Rate** matches your booking.

## Important tips
*   **Digital Contracts:** Many agencies use iPads now. Don't just tap "Accept". Scroll through and read the summary screen first.
*   **Keep a Copy:** Always ask for a PDF copy to be emailed to you immediately.
