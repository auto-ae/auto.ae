# Renting: Daily vs Monthly Rentals

## Overview
One of the unique features of the UAE rental market is the popularity of **Monthly Rentals** (or "mini-lease"). This page compares the financial and practical differences between short-term daily rentals and long-term monthly commitments.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Mini-Lease** | Treating a monthly rental as a substitute for owning a car. You pay month-to-month with no long-term bank loan. |
| **Pro-Rata Refund** | If you return a monthly rental early (e.g., after 2 weeks), most agencies will recalculate the price at the *Daily* rate, meaning you get very little refund. |

## Important tips
*   **The "Break-Even" Point:** Usually, if you need a car for more than 18-20 days, it is cheaper to book a Monthly plan than to pay the Daily rate for 3 weeks.
*   **Extension:** You can usually extend a Daily rental to a Monthly one by calling the agency, but agree on the rate *before* the first week ends.
