# Safety & Trust: Reporting Fraud

## Overview
If you see something suspicious, say something. Reporting bad actors helps us keep **AUTO.AE** clean. This guide explains how to report a user and what happens after you click that button.

## Important tips
*   **Don't Engage:** If someone insults you, don't insult them back. Just report and block. If you insult back, you might both get banned.
*   **Save Screenshots:** If they delete their account, the chat might disappear. Save screenshots as evidence for the Police.
