# Safety & Trust: Trust Checklist

## Overview
Print this page. Keep it in your pocket. Before you hand over 50,000 AED to a stranger, check every box. If you can't check a box, **STOP**.

## The 10 Commandments

### Pre-Meeting
- [ ] **Research Price:** I know the market value of this car.
- [ ] **Check VIN:** I have Googled the VIN and checked for US salvage history.
- [ ] **Call First:** I have spoken to the seller on the phone (not just WhatsApp).

### The Meeting
- [ ] **Public Place:** We are meeting at a petrol station or RTA.
- [ ] **ID Check:** I have seen the seller's Emirates ID and it matches the name on the Mulkiya.
- [ ] **Cold Start:** I heard the engine start from stone cold.

### The Deal
- [ ] **Inspection:** I have a PPI report or I am a mechanic myself.
- [ ] **Test Drive:** I drove the car for at least 15 minutes (Highway & City).
- [ ] **No Cash in Hand:** I will only Transfer money inside the RTA after the transfer is approved.
- [ ] **Keys:** I have tested BOTH keys (remotes often die).
