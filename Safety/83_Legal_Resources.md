## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Consumer Rights** | The Department of Economic Development (DED) protects you if you buy from a *Shops/Dealer*. They generally DO NOT interfere in private disputes between two individuals. |
| **Civil Case** | A court case to get your money back. It costs time and fees. |

## Official Channels

### 1. Dubai Police e-Crime
*   **Website:** `https://www.dubaipolice.gov.ae`
*   **Use for:** Cybercrimes (Hacking, Blackmail, Online Fraud).
*   **Cost:** Free.

### 2. DED Consumer Rights (600 54 5555)
*   **Use for:** Complaints against a Showroom or Rental Company.
*   **Example:** "Showroom sold me a car with a rolled-back odometer."

### 3. Rental Dispute Center
*   **Use for:** Disputes over long-term rental contracts or unfair charges.

## Important tips
*   **Save Everything:** Police need evidence. Screenshots of WhatsApp chats, voice notes, and bank transfer receipts.
*   **Don't Threaten:** Never threaten the scammer ("I will kill you", "I will deport you"). This can be used against YOU in court. Just file the case silently.
