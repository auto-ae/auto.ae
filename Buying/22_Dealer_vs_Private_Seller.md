# Buying: Dealer vs Private Seller

## Overview
One of the first choices you make on **AUTO.AE** is whether to buy from a business (Showroom/Dealer) or an individual (Private Seller). Each has distinct advantages and risks. This guide compares them so you can decide which suits your comfort level.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Showroom** | A registered business with a trade license. |
| **Private Seller** | An individual selling their personal car. |
| **Consumer Rights** | Laws that protect you when buying from a business (e.g., warranty against hidden defects), which do not apply to private sales. |

## Important tips
*   **For First-Time Buyers:** A Showroom is safer because they handle the paperwork and often provide a warranty.
*   **For Bargain Hunters:** Private sellers offer the best deals, but you must invest in a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection.
