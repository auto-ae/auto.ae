| **Marketplace** | A digital platform where multiple third-party sellers (individuals or dealers) list items for buyers. |
| **GCC** | Gulf Cooperation Council. AUTO.AE serves this region, including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, etc. |
| **VIP Plates** | Unique, low-number, or special pattern license plates that act as prestige assets in the UAE. |
| **Verified Showroom** | A professional car dealership that has passed AUTO.AE's verification checks. |

## Core Features

AUTO.AE is not just a listing site; it is an ecosystem with four distinct pillars:

### 1. Car Sales (Buy & Sell)
Users can list their own cars or browse thousands of listings from trusted dealerships. The platform supports advanced filtering by trim, specs, and regional specs (GCC vs. Import).

### 2. Car Rentals
A dedicated section for daily and monthly car rentals. Users can compare offers from multiple rental agencies, check insurance terms, and book transparently.

### 3. VIP Number Plates
A unique marketplace for trading prestigious license plates. Sellers can list plates from Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and other emirates, and buyers can find investment-grade numbers.

### 4. Showroom & Dealer Tools
Professional tools for dealerships to manage inventory, track leads, and build a reputation page to attract more local buyers.

## Important tips
*   **Use the App:** The mobile app often sends push notifications for price drops on your favorite cars.
*   **Check "Verified":** Look for the "Verified" badge on Showrooms to ensure you are dealing with a licensed business.
*   **GCC Specs:** When buying, always check if the car has "GCC Specs" to ensure it is built for the region's hot climate.
