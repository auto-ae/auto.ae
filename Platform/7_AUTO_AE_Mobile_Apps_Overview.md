# Platform: AUTO.AE Mobile Apps Overview

## Overview
While the **AUTO.AE** website offers a full desktop experience, the official mobile apps for iOS and Android are designed for speed and on-the-go access. The apps include exclusive features like push notifications for price drops and easier photo uploading for sellers.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Push Notification** | An instant alert sent to your phone screen (e.g., "The car you liked just dropped in price"). |
| **Biometric Login** | Using FaceID or Fingerprint to log in securely without typing a password. |

## App Features

The mobile app includes everything from the website, plus:

1.  **Instant Notifications:**
    *   Get alerted when a seller replies to your chat.
    *   Get notified if a saved car is sold or reduced in price.
2.  **Easy listing creation:**
    *   Take photos directly with your phone camera and upload them to your listing in seconds.
3.  **Location Services:**
    *   Find showrooms near your current GPS location.
4.  **Dark Mode:**
    *   Fully optimized dark mode for night-time browsing.
