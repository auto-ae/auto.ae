# Showrooms: Finding Dealers

## Overview
Sometimes you want to browse a showroom to see 50 cars in one afternoon rather than driving across town to see one. **AUTO.AE** allows you to search for dealerships by location and specialization.

## Using the Dealer Directory

### 1. The "Showrooms" Tab
Click on **"Showrooms"** in the main navigation bar. This opens the Dealer Directory.

### 2. Filters
*   **Location:** "Dubai", "Abu Dhabi", "Sharjah".
*   **Specialty:** Select tags like "Luxury", "Commercial Trucks", or "JDM Imports".

### 3. Dealer Profile
Clicking a dealer card shows:
*   Their full inventory list.
*   Google Maps location.
*   Working hours.
*   WhatsApp contact.

## Important tips
*   **Visit in Person:** Photos are great, but showroom visits allow you to negotiate better. Dealers prefer face-to-face deals.
*   **Check "Cars for Sale":** Before driving there, check their profile on AUTO.AE to see if they actually have stock in your budget.
