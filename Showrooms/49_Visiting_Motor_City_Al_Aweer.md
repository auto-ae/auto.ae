# Showrooms: Visiting Motor City / Al Aweer

## Overview
While the AUTO.AE app is great for browsing, sometimes you just want to walk through rows of 1000+ cars. Dubai and Sharjah have massive dedicated zones where hundreds of showrooms are clustered together. This guide helps you navigate these "Car Cities".

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tasjeel / Adnoc** | These are government testing centers located *inside* the car markets. You can buy a car, test it, insure it, and register it without leaving the complex. |
| **Export Plates** | Blue plates issued instantly at these markets for driving a car out of the country. |

## The Main Hubs

### 1. Al Aweer Exchange / DAZ (Dubai)
*   **Best for:** Used luxury cars, GCC specs family SUVs, and massive variety.
*   **Vibe:** Organized, clean, with huge air-conditioned showrooms.
*   **Location:** Ras Al Khor Industrial Area.

### 2. Souq Al Haraj (Sharjah)
*   **Best for:** Budget cars, older models, and export deals.
*   **Vibe:** Crowded, fast-paced, haggling is mandatory.
*   **Location:** Behind Tasjeel Village, Sharjah.

### 3. Motor World (Abu Dhabi)
*   **Best for:** High-end GCC specs cars favored by locals (Land Cruisers, Patrols).
*   **Location:** Al Shamkha area.

## Important tips
*   **Wear Comfortable Shoes:** These markets are kilometers long. You will walk a lot.
*   **Go During the Week:** Friday evenings and Sundays are packed. Tuesday morning is the best time for serious attention from sellers.
