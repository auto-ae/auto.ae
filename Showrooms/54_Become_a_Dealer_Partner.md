## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Lead** | A customer who clicked "Call" or "WhatsApp" on your listing. |
| **Qualified Lead** | A customer who has been verified by OTP (SMS) to ensure they are real human beings, not bots. |

## How to Join

### 1. Register Interest
Go to the footer of the site and click "For Dealeers". Fill out the contact form.

### 2. Submit Documents
Our onboarding team will ask for:
*   Trade License (DED).
*   Tax Registration Number (TRN) Certificate.
*   Emirates ID of the owner.

### 3. Choose a Package
*   **Standard:** up to 20 cars.
*   **Professional:** up to 100 cars + Featured Listings.
*   **Enterprise:** Unlimited cars + API integration with your own website.
