# Guides & Glossary: Glossary N-Z

## N
*   **No Plate:** License Plate.
*   **Non-GCC:** Any car imported from US, Japan, Korea, or Europe.

## O
*   **Odometer:** The gauge showing total distance driven.
*   **Original Paint:** A car that has never been repainted. Highly desirable.

## P
*   **Passing:** The RTA Vehicle Inspection Test. "The car passed."
*   **PPI (Pre-Purchase Inspection):** Paying a garage to check a car before buying.

## R
*   **Rollover:** When a car flips over in an accident. Total loss.
*   **RTA:** Road and Transport Authority (Dubai).

## S
*   **Salik:** The toll gate system in Dubai. The tag on your windshield.
*   **Salvage:** A car that was written off by insurance (usually in the US) and rebuilt. Dangerous.
*   **Service Contract:** A prepaid package for oil changes at the agency.

## T
*   **Third Party Insurance:** Covers only the *other* car if you cause an accident. Cheaper.
*   **Total Loss:** A car too damaged to be repaired.
*   **Transfer:** Moving ownership from Seller to Buyer.

## V
*   **VAT:** Value Added Tax (5%). Applied to dealer sales and service fees.
*   **VIN:** Vehicle Identification Number. 17 digits.

## W
*   **Warranty:** A guarantee to fix the car if it breaks.
*   **Write-Off:** Same as Total Loss.
