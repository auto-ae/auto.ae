# Guides & Glossary: Emergency Numbers

## Overview
Save these numbers in your phone now. When you are stuck on the hard shoulder of Sheikh Zayed Road at 3 AM, you don't want to be Googling.

## Police & Ambulance

### 1. Police (Emergency): 999
*   **Use when:** Life-threatening accident. Someone is injured. Car is on fire.
*   **Note:** Operators speak English, Arabic, Urdu, and more.

### 2. Police (Non-Emergency): 901
*   **Use when:** Minor fender bender. Blocking a driveway. Noise complaint.

### 3. Ambulance: 998
*   **Use when:** Medical emergency only.

### 4. Fire: 997

## Roadside Assistance
*   **AAA (Arabian Automobile Association):** 800 4900
*   **Recovery (Towing):** There are many private companies. 
    *   *Tip:* Keep one business card of a recovery truck driver in your glovebox.

## Traffic Reporting
*   **Dubai Police App:** Use the "Report Minor Accident" feature if no one is hurt. You don't need to wait for a patrol car.
*   **Rafid (Sharjah):** 800 72343.
