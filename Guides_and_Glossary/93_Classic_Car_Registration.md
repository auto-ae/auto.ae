# Guides & Glossary: Classic Car Registration

## Overview
Driving a 1967 Mustang in Dubai is a dream. But registering a car older than 30 years requires a special process. You can't just go to any testing center.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Classic Plate** | A special brown license plate. It looks prestigious. |
| **mileage Cap** | Classic cars are often limited to 10,000 km per year. You cannot use them as a daily taxi. |

## The 'Brown Plate' Process
1.  **Visit Tasjeel Al Barsha:** This is the main center for classics.
2.  **Safety Test:** They check brakes/lights, but they are lenient on emissions.
3.  **Visual Inspection:** The car must look good. No rust or massive dents.

## Important tips
*   **Insurance:** Normal insurance companies will reject you. You need specialist "Classic Car Insurance". It is cheap because they know you drive slowly and rarely.
*   **Air Conditioning:** Retrofitting a modern AC is allowed and highly recommended.
