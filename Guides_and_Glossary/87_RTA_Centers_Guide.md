# Guides & Glossary: RTA Centers Guide

## Overview
To buy, sell, or register a car in Dubai, you must visit an RTA (Roads and Transport Authority) center. There are many, but they offer different services.

## Major Centers (Tasjeel & Shamil)

### 1. Tasjeel Al Barsha (The Big One)
*   **Location:** Sheikh Zayed Road.
*   **Best for:** Buying luxury cars, Import tests, VIP service.
*   **Crowd:** Very Busy. Go early (7 AM).

### 2. Tasjeel Al Qusais
*   **Location:** Beirut Street.
*   **Best for:** Heavy vehicles, trucks, and classic cars.

### 3. Shamil Al Muhaisnah
*   **Location:** Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road.
*   **Best for:** Quick testing. Usually less crowded than Barsha.

### 4. Wasel Belhasa (Nad Al Hamar)
*   **Location:** Nad Al Hamar.
*   **Best for:** Deira residents.

## Services Offered
*   **Vehicle Testing:** 170 AED. Checks brakes, emissions, chassis.
*   **Registration:** 400 AED. issuing the Mulkiya and plates.
*   **Insurance:** Most centers have insurance brokers inside.
*   **Plate Factory:** Some centers can print plates on-site (Barsha/Qusais).

## Opening Hours
*   **Weekdays:** 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM.
*   **Fridays:** 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM (Some centers).
*   **Sundays:** Closed.
