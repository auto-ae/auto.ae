# Guides & Glossary: Community Guidelines

## Overview
**AUTO.AE** is a community of car lovers. To keep it fun and safe for everyone, we have a few simple rules. Breaking them means getting banned (and losing your Listings).

## The 3 Golden Rules

### 1. Be Honest
*   Don't hide accidents.
*   Don't roll back odometers.
*   Don't use fake photos.

### 2. Be Real
*   One account per person.
*   Don't use bots to scrape data.
*   Don't post the same car 5 times to spam the search results.

### 3. Be Nice
*   Treat buyers and sellers with respect.
*   No racism, sexism, or abusive language in chat.

## Forbidden Content
*   **Items:** Weapons, Alcohol, drugs.
*   **Services:** Promoting "Visa Services", "Massage", or "Crypto Scams".
*   **External Links:** Don't post links to competitor websites.

## Respect Policy
*   "Lowballing" is annoying ("Last price??"), but it is not illegal. Just ignore them.
*   Harassment (messaging 50 times after being blocked) constitutes Cyberbullying and will be reported to Police.
