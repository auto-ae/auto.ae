# Listings Quality: Featured Listings

## Overview
10,000 cars are uploaded to **AUTO.AE** every week. Your listing can easily get buried on Page 5 within a few days. **Featured Listings** allow you to pay a small fee to pin your car to the top of the search results.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Sticky Post** | A listing that stays at the top of the search results page, ignoring the chronological order. |
| **Badge** | A "Featured" label on the listing card that catches the buyer's eye. |

## The Featured Tiers

### 1. Silver Boost (99 AED / week)
*   **Effect:** Refreshes your ad to the top once a day.
*   **Best for:** Cars priced 20k - 50k AED (fast movers).

### 2. Gold Boost (299 AED / week)
*   **Effect:** Pins your ad to the top of the "Toyota" page.
*   **Best for:** Cars priced 50k - 150k AED.

### 3. Platinum Turbo (999 AED / month)
*   **Effect:** Appears on the **Homepage** and in "Recommended for You" emails.
*   **Best for:** Luxury cars (500k+ AED) that need a specific buyer.

## Important tips
*   **Don't Boost a Bad Ad:** Paying for visibility on a listing with 1 blurry photo is a waste of money. Fix the photos first.
*   **Timing:** Activate your boost on Thursday afternoon. The weekend traffic (Fri-Sun) is the highest.
