# VIP Plates: Plate Listing Page

## Overview
A Plate Listing on **AUTO.AE** is simple but contains high-value details. Since you are buying a digital asset, the listing focuses on the **Transferability** and the **Design** of the plate.

## Key concepts explained
| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Private Transfer** | The seller wants to meet you at the RTA office to sign the transfer papers manually. Common for very expensive plates (100k+ AED). |
| **Instant Online Transfer** | The seller has authorized the plate for digital trading. You can buy it through the app, and it moves to your traffic file immediately. |

## The Listings Detail

### 1. The Plate Graphic
*   The large image shows exactly how the plate looks.
*   *Note:* Pay attention to the **Code**. "A 123" is worth 10x more than "W 123". Don't just look at the number.

### 2. Price and Status
*   **Asking Price:** The seller's target.
*   **Make an Offer:** Most plate sellers expect negotiation. Use this button to send a counter-offer.

### 3. Seller Location
*   A plate listed in "Dubai" usually means the ownership file is in Dubai.
*   *Warning:* If you live in Abu Dhabi, buying a Dubai plate requires you to open a file in Dubai RTA.

## Important tips
*   **Screenshot the Listing:** Before you buy, keep a record of the listing in case of any dispute about the agreed price or logo inclusion.
*   **Check the "Hidden" Digits:** Some sellers blur the last digit to prevent spam. Message them to reveal the full number before making an offer.
